Venue

Charles K Kao Auditorium, Hong Kong Science Park, Shatin.
沙田香港科學園高錕會議中心

Thanks for Hong Kong Science and Technology Park Corp. Ltd. to sponsor the venue for Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2015.

Transportation

From University MTR Station

  1. Goto University MTR Station on East Rail Line
  2. Take Exit B at University MTR Station, you will find a bus terminal.
  3. Take KMB bus 272K to Hong Kong Science Park.

Remarks: Frequency of 272K on Saturday 07:50 - 09:30 is 6-8 minutes, and 09:30 - 11:00 is 10 minutes.

From Shatin MTR Station

  1. Goto Shatin MTR Station on East Rail Line
  2. Take Exit B at Shatin MTR Station, turn left and walk for about 30 metres, you will find a ramp going down. (To find this way, you can also look for a IKEA sign, the mini-bus station is at Pai Tau Street, near to IKEA)
  3. You will find a mini-bus station after going down from ramp.
  4. Take mini-bus route no. 27 (English) to Hong Kong Science Park.

Special Bus Services on Peak Hours

From Tsuen Wan West MTR Station (West Rail Line): 43P (Departing at 0723 / 0738 / 0753 / 0808 on Mondays to Fridays, and 0730 / 0750 / 0800 only on Saturdays)
From Diamond Hill MTR Station (Kwun Tong Line): 272S (Departing at 0725 - 0855 only on Mondays to Fridays, and 0730 / 0800 / 0820 / 0835 / 0845 only on Saturdays)
(please double check for above bus schedule from KMB website)

Looking for Hotel ?

Few hotels are available in Shatin area, which will closer to venue, and they are reachable by MTR with 10-min walk distance. You may also consider some hotel in Kowloon and Hong Kong Island near MTR stations (eg. Central to Prince on MTR Tsuen Wan Line, Hunghom to Kowloon Tong on MTR East Rail Line, Central to Tin Hau on Island Line, etc), which are 15-45 minutes on MTR travel to University Station from above examples.

Bus route 272K will arrive Hong Kong Science Park within 10 minutes after departure from University MTR Station bus terminus.

Oversea participants can look for hotels from online hotel websites. For enquiry, please feel freely to contact with conference team by email to conf2015@opensource.hk.
